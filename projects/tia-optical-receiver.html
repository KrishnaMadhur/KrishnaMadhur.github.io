Wideband Transimpedance Amplifier for Optical Receivers

Cadence Virtuoso · 45nm CMOS · Fall 2025

Designed an 8-stage cascaded transimpedance amplifier (TIA) for optical receiver front-ends, targeting high transimpedance gain with wide bandwidth for photodiode interfacing.

Performance Highlights

  • 519 kΩ (114 dBΩ) transimpedance gain — exceeding specification by 3.5×
  • 311 MHz bandwidth maintained with a 600Ω differential load
  • 3.5 mW power consumption — well below the 10 mW budget

Design Approach

The "Direct Coupling" strategy uses tapered loading across stages to eliminate AC coupling capacitors, improving both area efficiency and low-frequency response. Each stage was optimized for gain-bandwidth product with careful sizing of the feedback resistor.
